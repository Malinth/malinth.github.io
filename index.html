About me: A digital guru

I am a creative person, who loves web development and web designing. I have had a passion for photography and travelling over the past 13 years. I am born and raised in beautiful Sweden, and so far I have lived in 5 different cities, travelled in 6 different countries and today I am a digital nomad - or a digital guru, as I also call myself.

I have a certificate in web development for e-commerce, with focus on web development in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Liquid, system development in PHP, Wordpress, Magento, Shopify, SEO, Google Analytics and digital marketing as well as social media content management.
